Fiberglass manufacturing demands highly active external lubricants and specialty PVC plasticizers. Our products prevent compound adhesion to structural molds, reduce viscosity during extrusion, and increase mechanical shear limits during heavy-duty forming operations.

B2B Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Review standard terms of trade, lead times, customization rules, and quality control baselines.

What is the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) for custom PVC compounds?

For custom PVC Compound formulations (footwear soles, specialized wire cabling insulation), our standard B2B minimum order is 1 Metric Ton (MT). For standard off-the-shelf catalog compounds, we can occasionally arrange smaller test dispatches starting at 500 kg.

Do you provide REACH and RoHS compliance certificates?

Yes. Khera Chemical Industries guarantees that all stabilizer compounds, external lubricants, and plasticizers are fully REACH and RoHS compliant. Batch-specific Certificates of Analysis (COA) and Technical Datasheets (TDS) are supplied with every cargo shipment.

What is your standard production and shipment lead time?

Standard PVC plasticizer and lubricant orders are dispatched within 3-5 working days from our Bahadurgarh plant. Specialized liquid stabilizer mixes and custom footwear compound orders take 7-10 working days, depending on reactor allocation schedules.

Can you formulate low-toxic or bio-based plasticizers?

Yes. In response to recent health and safety updates in B2B packaging and toy manufacturing, we offer high-grade eco-friendly DOTP (Dioctyl Terephthalate) and bio-based ester plasticizers as substitutes for phthalate-based DOP plasticizers.

What packaging formats are available for bulk liquids?

Liquid PVC plasticizers and stabilizers are packaged in standard 200L HDPE/Steel Drums, 1000L IBC Containers, or loaded directly into multi-ton Flexitanks and bulk tank trucks for high-volume domestic extruders.

How do you manage quality control discrepancies?

We archive representative chemical retention samples from every shipment for 24 months. If a quality query arises, we cross-analyze the retained sample with the client's material feedback, resolving disputes with rapid replacement or laboratory compensation.
